TKG

TKG, or tamagokakegohan, means raw eggs over rice in Japanese. This meal is incredibly simple and quick to make, and delicious! I eat it for breakfast almost every day! Here are the instructions of how to make it!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of raw rice
  • water
  • 3 eggs
  • furikake(optional)
  • soy sauce(optional)
  • kimchi(optional)
