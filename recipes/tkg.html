TKG

TKG, or tamagokakegohan, means raw eggs over rice in Japanese. This meal is incredibly simple and quick to make, and delicious! I eat it for breakfast almost every day! Here are the instructions of how to make it!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of raw rice
  • water
  • 3 eggs
  • furikake(optional)
  • soy sauce(optional)
  • kimchi(optional)

Instructions

  1. Using a rice cooker bowl, add 1 cup of rice using a measuring cup.
  2. Fill up the bowl with water, then wash the rice, emptying the murky water.
  3. Repeat step two several times, until the water is clear.
  4. Fill up the rice bowl with water up to the 1 cup line, then cook the rice.
  5. When rice is finished cooking, scoop the rice into a bowl.
  6. crack 3 eggs into the bowl
  7. add some optional seasonings, like soy sauce, kimchi, or some furikake
  8. Mix all the ingredients together. You are now ready to enjoy eating some TKG!!
