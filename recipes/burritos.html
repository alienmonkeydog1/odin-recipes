Burrito Recipe!!

For this recipe, I will talk about how to make burritos easily and at home. This burrito recipe will only use ingredients easily found in supermarkets in Japan, because that's where I live. I hope you enjoy following and making this recipe, and learn something in the process!

Ingredients

  • torillas
  • mexican/shredded cheese
  • tomatoes
  • meat
  • beans
  • onions
  • bell pepper

Instructions

  1. cut the onions, bell pepper, and beans on a cutting board
  2. Using some oil, fry the onions, bell pepper, and meat, adding salt
  3. heat up some tortillas on the frying pan
  4. On a plate, fill each tortilla up with cooked onions, bell pepper, meat, tomatoes, and cheese.
  5. Fold and wrap the tortilla into a burrito. Enjoy!
