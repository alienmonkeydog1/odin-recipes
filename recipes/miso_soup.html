Miso Soup Recipe

This recipe describes how to make a simple miso soup that uses a lot of vegetables. It is incredibly healthy, and is good for you. This is a one pot recipe, so the longest part of the process is creating the dashi, or soup stock.

Ingredients

  • miso
  • hokusai
  • carrots
  • chicken thighs
  • green onions
  • daikon
  • tofu
  • konbu
  • katsuoboshi

Instructions

  1. Fill a pot with water, and add 3-4 pieces of konbu. Let the water sit, up to 12 hours.
  2. Heat up the water to a low boil. Add a generous amount of katsuoboshi.
  3. Steep the katsuoboshi for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Cut up the carrots, daikon, hokusai, and tofu. Remember to wash and peel the hokusai, carrots, and daikon.
  5. Add all of the ingredients to the soup, and put the lid on. Boil on medium high for around 30 minutes.
  6. Test the carrots texture for ideal cookedness. If satisfied, turn the heat off.
  7. Wait several minutes for the soup to cool, then add miso to taste.
